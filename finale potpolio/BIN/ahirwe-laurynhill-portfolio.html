Biography

Versatile. Compelling.
Unforgettable.

Ahirwe Laurynhill is a Ugandan actress, singer, beauty queen, and model with a deep passion for storytelling through film. Her approach to every character is rooted in thorough research, strong script analysis, and a commitment to authenticity.

Born 22 November 2002 in Uganda, she has built an impressive portfolio across television series, short films, and commercial campaigns — earning recognition for both her talent and her professionalism.

Laurynhill Foundation Uganda (LFUg)

The Laurynhill Foundation Uganda is a community-driven initiative dedicated to uplifting and empowering young people across Uganda — with a special focus on youth in the arts, education access, and social wellbeing.

Rooted in the belief that every young person deserves opportunity, the foundation channels resources, mentorship, and advocacy toward building a stronger generation of Ugandan talent.
